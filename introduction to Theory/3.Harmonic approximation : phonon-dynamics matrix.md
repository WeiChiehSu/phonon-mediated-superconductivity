C 稱為諧和力常數

C is called the harmonic force constant.

$$
原子間存在諧和勢的一維原子鍊,第j個cell內有兩個原子j,1和j,2,質量為m_1和m_2,偏移平衡點的位移為u_{j_{1} }和u_{j_{2} },其Kinetic energy和potential為:
$$

$$
For{}a{}one-dimensional{}atomic{}chain{}with{}harmonic{}interactions{}between{}atoms,{}each{}unit{}cell{}j{}contains{}two{}atoms,{}j,1{}and{}j,2,{}with{}masses{}m_1{}and{}m_2.{}Their{}displacements{}from{}the{}equilibrium{}positions{}are{}u_{j_1}{}and{}u_{j_2},{}respectively.{}The{}kinetic{}energy{}and{}potential{}energy{}are{}given{}by:
$$

$$
Kinetic-energy:\sum_{j}^{} (\frac{m_{1} }{2} \dot{u}_{j,1}^{2} + \frac{m_{2} }{2} \dot{u}_{j,2}^{2})
$$

$$
potential:\sum_{j}^{} \frac{c}{2} [(u_{j,1}- u_{j-1,2})^{2}+(u_{j,2}- u_{j,1})^{2} +(u_{j+1,1}- u_{j,2})^{2}]   
$$

c代表force constant,計算該系統的Lagrangian(L),並解Lagrange equation:

Here, C denotes the force constant. Calculate the Lagrangian (L) of the system and solve the Lagrange equations:

$$
L=K-U;Lagrange-equation:\frac{d}{dt} \frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{r} } =\frac{\partial L}{\partial r}
$$

得該系統的兩個原子j,1和j,2的運動方程:

This yields the equations of motion for the two atoms j,1 and j,2 in the system:

$$
j,1: -m_{1}\ddot{u}_{j,1} = c[ {u}_{j-1,2}-2{u}_{j,1}+{u}_{j,2}]
$$

$$
j,2: -m_{2}\ddot{u}_{j,2} = c[ {u}_{j,1}-2{u}_{j,2}+{u}_{j+1,1}]
$$


設運動方程的解為平面波:

Assume that the solutions of the equations of motion are plane waves:

$$
\begin{cases}
u_{j,1}(t) = A_1 e^{i(q j a - \omega_q t)}, & \ddot{u}_{j,1}(t) = -\omega_q^2 A_1 e^{i(q j a - \omega_q t)} \\
u_{j,2}(t) = A_2 e^{i(q j a - \omega_q t)}, & \ddot{u}_{j,2}(t) = -\omega_q^2 A_2 e^{i(q j a - \omega_q t)}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
第j\pm 1個晶格內第n個原子的平面波解為:
$$

$$
The{}plane-wave{}solution{}for{}the{}n-th{}atom{}in{}the{}(j{}\pm{}1)-th{}unit{}cell{}is:

$$
u_{j\pm 1,n}=A_{n}e^{i[q(j\pm 1)a-\omega _{q}t] }=A_{n}e^{\pm iqa}   e^{i[qja-\omega _{q}t] }

$$
a為晶格的間距,將{u}_{j,n}和\ddot{u}_{j,1}代入運動方程中,並寫成矩陣形式:
$$

$$
Let{}a{}be{}the{}lattice{}spacing.{}Substituting{}{u}_{j,n}{}and{}\ddot{u}_{j,1}{}into{}the{}equations{}of{}motion,{}we{}can{}write{}them{}in{}matrix{}form:
$$

$$
\begin{cases}
(2c - \omega_q^2 m_1) A_1 - c(e^{-iqa} + 1) A_2 = 0 \\

\end{cases}
\;\Longrightarrow\;
\begin{bmatrix}
2c - \omega_q^2 m_1 & -c(e^{-iqa}+1) \\
