Use Wick’s theorem:

$$
 <C_{n,\vec{k} _{1}\mp \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n,\vec{k} _{2}\pm \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n{'} ,\vec{k} _{1}}C_{n^{'} ,\vec{k} _{2}}>=<C_{n,\vec{k} _{1}\mp \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n{'} ,\vec{k} _{1}}><C_{n,\vec{k} _{2}\pm \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n^{'} ,\vec{k} _{2}}>
 $$

引入粒子密度:

Introduce the particle density:

$$
g(n)=C_{n,k}^{+}C_{n^{},k^{}}
$$

算符將變換為粒子密度形式:

The operator is transformed into the particle density form:

$$
<C_{n,\vec{k} _{1}\mp \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n{'} ,\vec{k} _{1}}><C_{n,\vec{k} _{2}\pm \vec{q}}^{+}C_{n^{'} ,\vec{k} _{2}}>=g_{electron_{1}} (\vec{k})g_{electron_{2}}(\vec{k})

電子-聲子耦合能量修正將變換為:

The electron–phonon coupling energy Correction is transformed into:

$$
\left \langle \phi_{k}|H_{ep}^{total} |\phi_{n}  \right \rangle= \sum_{qknn^{'}}^{}\frac{2\hbar \omega _{q} |g_{nn^{'}}(k,q)|^{2}}{(E(\vec{k } )-E(\vec{k'}))^{2}-(\hbar \omega _{q})^{2}}g_{electron_{1}} (\vec{k})g_{electron_{2}}(\vec{k})

$$
根據BCS理論,庫柏對只在費米面附近薄層產生,因此以費米能級\epsilon _{F}為基準, g_{electron} (\vec{k}) -> g_{electron}(\epsilon _{F}),替換為電子在費米能級的態密度:
$$

$$
According{}to{}BCS{}theory,{}Cooper{}pairs{}form{}only{}within{}a{}thin{}layer{}near{}the{}Fermi{}surface.{}Therefore,{}taking{}the{}Fermi{}energy{} \epsilon _{F}{}as{}the{}reference,{}we{}can{}approximate{}g_{electron}{}(\vec{k}){}->{}g_{electron}(\epsilon _{F}),{}replacing{}it{}with{}the{}electronic{}density{}of{}states{}at{}the{}Fermi{}level.
$$

$$
\left \langle \phi_{k}|H_{ep}^{total} |\phi_{n}  \right \rangle= \sum_{qknn^{'}}^{}\frac{2\hbar \omega _{q} |g_{nn^{'}}(k,q)|^{2}}{(E(\vec{k } )-E(\vec{k'}))^{2}-(\hbar \omega _{q})^{2}}g_{electron} (\epsilon _{F})g_{electron} (\epsilon _{F})
$$

根據電子態密度的定義:假設電子分佈在一系列的電子能級Ei，那麼只有當能量E為某一個Ei的時候才會有電子態存在，其他能量的電子態為0.因此態密度轉換成狄拉克函數的形式為:

According to the definition of the electronic density of states: assuming electrons are distributed over a series of energy levels Ei , there is an electron state only when the energy E equals one of the Ei ; at other energies, the density of states is zero. Therefore, the density of states can be expressed in the form of a Dirac delta function:

$$
g_{electron}(E_{f})=\frac{\Omega _{cell}}{(2\pi ^{3}) } \sum_{n}^{} \int_{BZ}^{}\delta (E-E_{f})dE
$$

$$
\Omega _{cell}為cell的體積,在BZ內積分.
$$

$$
\Omega _{cell}{}is{}the{}volume{}of{}the{}unit{}cell,{}and{}the{}integration{}is{}performed{}over{}the{}Brillouin{}zone{}(BZ).
$$

可以將態密度轉換成狄拉克函數的形式的原因為,狄拉克函數具有下面的性質:

The reason the density of states can be expressed in the form of a Dirac delta function is that the Dirac delta function has the following property:

$$
g(E_{f})=\int g(E)\delta (E-E_{f})dE和\int_{-\infty }^{\infty} \delta (E)dE=1
$$

因此電子-聲子耦合能量修正最終能變換成我們熟知的電子-聲子耦合係數:

Therefore, the electron–phonon coupling energy Correction can ultimately be transformed into the well-known electron–phonon coupling constant:

$$
\lambda _{q}=\frac{\sum_{nn^{'}}^{} \int_{BZ}^{} \left | g_{nn^{'}}(k,q) ^{2} \right | \delta (\epsilon _{n,K}-\epsilon _{F})\delta (\epsilon _{n^{'} ,k^{'}}-\epsilon _{F})d\epsilon}{g_{electron} (\epsilon _{n^{'} ,k^{'}}-\epsilon _{F})\omega _{q}\Omega _{BZ}} 
$$

$$
再除g_{electron} (\epsilon _{n^{'} ,k^{'}}-\epsilon _{F})一次目的是讓電子-聲子耦合係數無量綱化,我們可以將電子-聲子耦合能量修正簡化為下面的形式:
$$

$$
Dividing{}again{}by{}g_{electron}{}(\epsilon _{n^{'},k^{'}}-\epsilon _{F}){}serves{}to{}nondimensionalize{}the{}electron–phonon{}coupling{}constant.{}We{}can{}then{}simplify{}the{}electron–phonon{}coupling{}energy{}Correction{}into{}the{}following{}form:
$$

$$
\lambda _{q}=\sum_{nn^{'}}^{}  \left | g_{nn^{'}}(k,q) ^{2} \right | g_{electron} (\epsilon _{F})
$$

$$
顯而易見地,電子-聲子耦合係數的物理意義為受到頻率為\omega _{q}的一個聲子散射後,根據能量散射修正g_{nn^{'}}(k,q) ^{2},費米能級的電子能量因電子聲子耦合拉低了多少!!!!!
$$

$$
It{}is{}evident{}that{}the{}physical{}meaning{}of{}the{}electron–phonon{}coupling{}constant{}is:{}after{}scattering{}by{}a{}phonon{}with{}frequency{}\omega_{q},{}the{}energy{}of{}electrons{}at{}the{}Fermi{}level{}is{}lowered{}by{}how{}much{}due{}to{}electron–phonon{}coupling,{}as{}determined{}by{}the{}energy{}scattering{}correction{}g_{nn^{'}}(k,q)^{2}.
$$

$$
接下來要要乘上\omega _{q}的聲子數量,考慮頻率為\omega _{q}的聲子貢獻了多少電子能量修正:
$$

$$
Next,{}we{}need{}to{}multiply{}by{}the{}number{}of{}phonons{}at{}frequency{}\omega_{q},{}to{}consider{}how{}much{}electronic{}energy{}renormalization{}is{}contributed{}by{}phonons{}with{}frequency{}\omega_{q}:
$$

$$
這個公式定義為\alpha ^{2}F(\omega _{q} ),被稱為Eliashberg{}spectral{}function:
$$

$$
This{}formula{}is{}defined{}as{}\alpha ^{2}F(\omega _{q}),{}which{}is{}called{}the{}Eliashberg{}spectral{}function.
$$

$$
\alpha ^{2}F(\omega _{q} ) =\omega _{q}\lambda _{q}g_{phonon}(\omega _{q})
$$

$$
g_{phonon}(\omega _{q})為頻率為\omega _{q}的聲子態密度,也就是\omega _{q}的聲子數量
$$

$$
g_{phonon}(\omega _{q}){}is{}the{}phonon{}density{}of{}states{}at{}frequency{} \omega _{q},{}i.e.,{}the{}number{}of{}phonons{}with{}frequency{} \omega _{q}.
$$

同理,聲子態密度也能轉換成狄拉克函數的形式:

$$
\alpha ^{2}F(\omega _{q} ) =\frac{1}{2\Omega _{BZ} } \int_{BZ}^{} \omega _{q}\lambda _{q}\delta (\omega -\omega _{q})d\omega _{q}

$$
