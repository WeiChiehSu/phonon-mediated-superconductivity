From the expression, it is clear that the scattering amplitude depends on the energies of the states; therefore, it is necessary to define the energy changes of the initial state n, the intermediate state, and the final state k.

$$

\text{intermediate-state (1-release): }
\begin{cases}

\begin{cases}
\left| \phi_k \right\rangle

= \left| \vec{k}_1 - \vec{q}, \vec{k}_2 + \vec{q} ; 0 \right\rangle, \\

E_k = E(\vec{k}_1') + E(\vec{k}_2')
\end{cases}

\begin{cases}

\begin{cases}
