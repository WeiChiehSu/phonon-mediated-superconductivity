擁有系統的聲子頻率後,便能解出電子聲子耦合矩陣元:

Once the phonon frequencies of the system are obtained, the electron–phonon coupling matrix elements can be calculated:

$$
\langle n,k | G_q | n', k' \rangle=g_{nn^{'}}(k,q)
$$

$$
G_{q}=-\frac{1}{N_{p} }\frac{1}{\sqrt{2M\omega _{q}} }iqe^{i(k+q-k^{'} )r_{i} }V_{K-S}(q)
$$

$$
得到電子聲子耦合矩陣元和聲子頻率後,便能在得到以動量為變數的響應擾動勢\Delta V_{K-S}(q+G)後,引入微擾理論,用DFT解出的波函數和能量進行二階微擾計算,解出響應擾動勢對基態能量的修正期望值\Delta n(q+G)那步中,把公式變換成電子聲子耦合係數的公式:
$$

$$
After{}obtaining{}the{}electron–phonon{}coupling{}matrix{}elements{}and{}the{}phonon{}frequencies,{}the{}expression{}in{}the{}step{}where{}the{}induced{}perturbation{}potential{}\Delta{}V_{K-S}(q+G){}is{}used{}together{}with{}perturbation{}theory—employing{}the{}wavefunctions{}and{}energies{}obtained{}from{}DFT{}to{}perform{}a{}second-order{}perturbation{}calculation{}and{}determine{}the{}correction{}to{}the{}ground-state{}energy{}expectation{}value{}\Delta{}n(q+G)—can{}be{}reformulated{}into{}the{}expression{}for{}the{}electron–phonon{}coupling{}coefficient:
$$

$$
\frac{4}{N\Omega } \sum_{k}^{} \sum_{c,v}^{} \frac{\left \langle \psi_{v,k}  \right |e^{-i(q+G).r } \left | \psi_{c,k+q}   \right \rangle \left \langle \psi_{c,k+q} \right |\Delta V_{SCF}(q+G)\left | \psi_{v,k}   \right \rangle    }{\varepsilon_{v,k}-\varepsilon _{c,k+q}   }\Longrightarrow 2\hbar \omega _{q} \sum_{k}^{} \sum_{c,v}^{} \frac{\left \langle \psi_{v,k}  \right | |g_{nn^{'}}(k,q)|^{2}\left | \psi_{v,k}   \right \rangle    }{(\varepsilon_{v,k}-\varepsilon _{c,k+q})^{2}-(\hbar \omega _{q})^{2} } =\sum_{nn^{'}}^{}\frac{  \omega _{q}\left | G_{nn^{'}}(k,q) ^{2} \right | g_{electron} (\epsilon _{F})g_{electron^{'}} (\epsilon _{F})}{g_{electron^{'}} (\epsilon _{F})\omega _{q}\Omega _{BZ}}
$$

可以看到DFPT和電子聲子耦合係數的公式天生匹配,當你解出材料的力時,也有計算費米能級的電子態密度,便能順勢解出電子聲子耦合係數.

It can be seen that DFPT and the expression for the electron–phonon coupling coefficient are naturally compatible: once the forces in a material are obtained, the electronic density of states at the Fermi level is also evaluated, allowing the electron–phonon coupling coefficient to be computed straightforwardly.
